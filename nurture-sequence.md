## Email 2 — Value Add (Day 3)
**Subject:** The #1 mistake I see architects make on their websites

**Body:**
Hey [Name],

Finished reading the guide? I hope it gave you a clearer picture of what to expect.

Today I wanted to share something I see over and over: architects spending money on beautiful portfolio sites that don't actually generate leads.

The problem? Design-first sites often forget the "conversion" part — they look great but don't tell visitors what to do next.

A few quick signs your site might have this issue:
- No clear "Hire Me" or "Contact" CTA above the fold
- Portfolio images with no context about the project or outcome
- No social proof (testimonials, client logos, case studies)

Want me to take a quick look at your site and give you 2-3 specific suggestions? Just hit reply — I'd be happy to help.

Joe
